BarHop

Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap, Google Places API and deployed to Heroku

BarHop allows users to search a suburb or street and have a list of bars and nightclubs in that area be returned to them in a card format, to make choosing their next social outing easier. Users can also sign up to save bars they like.

Investment Calculator

React, Chart.js and the AlphaVantage API

When planning this project, I felt my grasp on React wasn't strong enough. I thought the best way to learn something is to just dive in and do it so that's what I did. This apps purpose is to chart the share prices of any american company across different time periods. I also added a second chart with a form that shows you what an investment in that company would look like today.
